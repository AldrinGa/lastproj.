Frequently Asked Questions



What kinds of baked goods do you sell?

We serve Filipino baked good staples and craft unique fusion creations, blending the richness of Filipino flavors with beloved international classics.

Are your products freshly baked?

Yes! All our baked goods are freshly made daily to ensure quality and authentic taste.

How do you pack baked goods for delivery?

We carefully pack our products in sealed, food-safe containers to maintain freshness during delivery.

How long do the baked goods stay fresh?

Most breads are best enjoyed within 2–3 days, while pastries and specialty items may last up to 5 days when stored properly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery (depending on your location).

How can I contact you if I have more questions?

ou can reach us via our Contact Us page, email at pinoycrumbs@gmail.com, or message us on our facebook page.
