The Story Behind Pinoy Crumbs

Founded with a deep love for Filipino flavors, Pinoy Crumbs began as a small idea to bring local baked goods closer to every Filipino here in the country. We believe that baked goods do more than fill stomachs, they carry memories, spark joy, and connect generations.

Our journey started with our parents’ puto business, which helped support us growing up. Inspired by their dedication and passion, as their children we become as equally passionate about food and innovation in this modern generation, we envision creating an e-commerce platform that makes it easier for people to discover and enjoy traditional Filipino bakes from the comfort of their homes.

By using technology, we’re able to support small local bakers and make their creations accessible across the country, faster, fresher, and more convenient than ever. Our goal is simple, to support local bakers and connect communities through the taste of tradition. Built by passionate students and inspired by home-style baking, our platform is a space where Filipino favorites can be celebrated, shared, and enjoyed with ease.

So join us as we rise one bake at a time.
